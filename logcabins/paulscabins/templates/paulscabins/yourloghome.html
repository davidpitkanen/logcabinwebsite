Planning Your Log Home

Many factors influence the planning of any log home. Your property will be a major consideration. We also encourage our clients to think about budget, lifestyle, needs and dreams. All come into play during the planning stage. The success of your design will be a marriage of these components.

Often our clients come to us with their plans in hand. If this is not the case, we work through the process with the client to solidify their dreams and create custom plans. If required the services of an engineer are available through one of our trusted contacts.

Services available from us include assistance formulating and fine tuning your plan into the home that not only is custom to you but that conforms to codes and other trades.

We have had the pleasure of many years and many wonderful clients that have contributed to filling our repertoire with innovative and unique ideas and results. Sometimes the impossible just takes a little longer. We attempt to match your imagination to our abilities and are very receptive to challenges!

Research into the company chosen to supply your needs is a mandatory facet of your successful build and we believe you will find our company to have both the skills and expertise.
