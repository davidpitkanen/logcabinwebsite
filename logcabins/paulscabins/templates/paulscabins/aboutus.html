About Us


R. J. Edelkoort Contracting was established in 1972 as a building contractor. In 1980 the shift to log home building occurred and has remained the primary focus ever since. Although a wide variety of techniques and products have been incorporated into our portfolio since that beginning, hand crafting remains our passion.

Our resume includes the replacement and restoration of rotting or damaged logs in old log structures.
