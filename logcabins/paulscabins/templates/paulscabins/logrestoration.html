Log Restoration

We are one of the few log home builders that also offer the service of replacing rotting or damaged logs in your building. Older log buildings were often built very close to the ground making them vulnerable to ground moisture and insect attack. Inadequate roof overhangs also contribute to the problem of log end rot.

Techniques have been developed that in most cases allow us to remove and replace rotted logs without major disruption to the building. Logs that show minor signs of rot can be stabilized and treated to prevent further deterioration.
